**Indepedent variables:**

- uid: the unique identified for an X-ray.

- MeSH: Medical Subject Hedical Subject Headings is a comprehensive controlled vocabulary.

- Problems: key words corresponding to the problem identified.

- image [DROPPED]: anonymized specifics of the type of imaging done. 

- indication [DROPPED]: what the patient has been diagnosed with/described as the problem.

- comparison [DROPPED]: (hypothesis: either a comparison to another patient in the past with a similar problem, or a comparison to an X-Ray made in the past on the same patient.

- filename: associated image.

- projection: Frontal or Lateral image.


**Dependent variables:**

- findings: input from doctor about findings made.

- impression: final impression made by doctor.
